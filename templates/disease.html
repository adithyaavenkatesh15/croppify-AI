What is Crop Disease Detection?

Crop disease detection refers to the process of identifying diseases that affect crops. Timely detection helps in preventing the spread of the disease and applying treatments to safeguard the crops.

Common Crop Diseases:

  • Leaf Spot Disease: A fungal infection that affects the leaves, leading to yellowing and premature leaf drop.
  • Powdery Mildew: A fungal disease that creates a white, powdery coating on leaves and stems.
  • Blight: Caused by bacteria or fungi, it leads to wilting, browning, and rot in plants.

This tool helps identify diseases in crops and provides treatment or prevention options.
